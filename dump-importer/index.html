WebRTC Dump Importer

Visualize dumps from chrome://webrtc-internals or rtcstats. See this blog post for a detailed walkthrough of the data.

Want more from your dump? rtcstats.com cuts through the noise to get you to a root cause fast - and makes WebRTC debugging accessible even if you're not a WebRTC expert. Quality scores, automatic observations, AI-powered analysis - free to use.

Accepts webrtc-internals JSON dumps and rtcstats dumps (.json, .txt, .gz)
