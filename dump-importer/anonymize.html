Need to anonymize your webrtc-internals or rtcstats dump file?
That's what we're here to do!


This downloads a copy of the webrtc-internals or rtcstats dump with IP addresses obfuscated in the following events:
  • candidate lines in the SDP in setLocalDescription, setRemoteDescription et al
  • ICE candidates (with the exception of TURN server / relay addresses)
  • statistics
The obfuscation replaces the last part of IPv4 and the last six parts of IPv6 with an 'x'.
This removes some of the privacy concern about uploading this e.g. to the Chromium issue tracker.
Why obfuscate and anonymize?
  • So you can safely upload the file as part of a chrome bug report - or anywhere else for that matter
  • This is part of what we do at rtcstats.com when we visualize your data... we don't own the data and don't want anything to do with the privacy of your users - we're here to help you troubleshoot and debug
